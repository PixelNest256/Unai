Calc Skill

This skill performs mathematical calculations and equation solving using SymPy.

Examples:
- Basic calculations: "3 + 4 * 2"
- Equation solving: "solve: x^2 - 4"
- Expression expansion: "expand: (x+1)^2"
- Complex expressions: "2*sin(pi/4) + sqrt(16)"

Supported operations:
- Basic arithmetic: +, -, *, /
- Trigonometric functions: sin, cos, tan
- Logarithms: log, ln
- Square roots: sqrt
- Equation solving with "solve:"
- Expression expansion with "expand:"
- And many more mathematical functions

The skill shows step-by-step solutions for complex calculations.
