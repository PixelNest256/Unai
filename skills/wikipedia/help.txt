Wikipedia Skill

This skill searches Wikipedia and provides article summaries.

Examples:
- "What is Python?"
- "Tell me about Albert Einstein"
- "アインシュタインについて"
- "Pythonとは何ですか"

The skill:
- Searches Wikipedia for relevant articles
- Provides concise summaries of articles
- Supports both English and Japanese queries
- Accesses only ja.wikipedia.org

Note: This skill requires internet connection to fetch Wikipedia data.
